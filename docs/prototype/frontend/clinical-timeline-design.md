# AI--Nursing Clinical Timeline Design

## Purpose

The clinical timeline organizes patient events in chronological order.

The goal is to help nurses quickly understand:

- What happened
- When it happened
- How the patient responded
- What actions were taken

The timeline connects data points into a clinical story.

---

# 1. Timeline Structure

Each event should include:

## Time

When did the event occur?

Example:

08:00

---

## Event

What happened?

Examples:

- Nursing assessment completed
- Medication administered
- Oxygen requirement increased
- Laboratory result changed

---

## Clinical Significance

Why may this matter?

Example:

"Increasing oxygen requirement may indicate worsening respiratory status."

---

## Response

What was done?

Examples:

- Reassessment completed
- Intervention initiated
- Provider notified
- Patient response documented
